My Background

Why am I taking this course at Epicodus?

I've always enjoyed programming. I self taught myself bits and pieces over most of my life. But I found, in every language, I would hit a wall. This wall would come after about two weeks. I never found a way past it. When I looked for jobs, I realized quickly that I had to get past the wall if I wanted to continue in this field. I asked around and found Epicodus is exactly what I needed. I believe, without a doubt, that the pair programming environment, and weekly code reviews, will allow me to break past the wall and become the best programmer in any language I can possibly be. And also hopefully I'll get hired at the end, but that's for future me to find out.
